# Changelog

## [1.0.0] - 2026-07-13
### Added
- Initial public release.
- Procedural crop bed generation with configurable rows, spacing, jitter, skip chance, and formula-driven curved rows.
- Multi-species weed scattering by area density.
- Procedural terrain: flat, grayscale heightmap, or NumPy noise base, plus optional additive ridge/furrow, anisotropic surface noise, and clod layers.
- Per-seed environmental randomization of lights and ground materials.
- Point-instancer and instanceable generation modes.
- Omniverse Replicator capture pipeline (RGB and semantic segmentation) with per-frame seed and camera randomization.
- Bundled sample corn and weed models with a ready-to-run default configuration.
